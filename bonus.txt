- Press 'F' to switch among 4 floor textures
	(1) Xor grayscale
	(2) Xor RGB
	(3) Xor HSV
	(4) Nep-Nep shows the way to true North

- Press 'M' to toggle minimap:
	(1) No minimap
	(2) Fixed minimap
	(3) Rotating minimap

- Press 'C' to toggle on/off the "fishbowl" distortion

- Press 'V' to toggle 'wobble mode'
	(1) No wobble
	(2) Quite wobble
	(3) Extreme wobble into mirror image

- Press 'Tilde' key to save a screenshot to ppm file

- Press 'Tab' to show/hide mouse cursor

- Drag and resize window with mouse cursor

- Press 'Spacebar' to shoot burgers

- Network multi-player up to 256 players!
